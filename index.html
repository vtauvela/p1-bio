VCD

I am a Visual Communication Design major. At first I was convinced that I would pursue a career in Applied Developmental Psychology. I then switched to Visual Arts, and from there I switched over to the VCD program, where I was able to combine my creative passion with principles and structural fundamentals that could prove to be marketable and beneficial to others.

I want my creativity to be more than a manifestation of my emotions and thoughts -- I wanted it to be harnessed in a way that could potentially benefit others. I love the concept of combining form and function, and how it has proven to be a vital contribution in today's workforce.

I am a former illustrator and graphic artist who worked for EWU's college paper, The Easterner. As far as strengths and weaknesses go, I'd say my strength as a VCD student would be information design and logo design/branding. My weakness would be web design and prepress.
