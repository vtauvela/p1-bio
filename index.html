I am a Visual Communication Design major. In high school, I was very passionate about art and track. When asked by my peers about what career path I wanted to pursue, graphic design was not at the forefront of my mind.

I am currently finishing up my major in VCD with a completed minor in Applied Developmental Psychology. Outside of school, I'm usually drawing, painting, reading the Bible, working out or taking naps whenever I can.

I am a former illustrator and graphic artist who worked for EWU's college paper, The Easterner. The areas that I tend to do well in include information design and logo design/branding. The area I'm really trying to focus on is understanding and practicing responsive web design, and overcoming shyness when presenting my work.
